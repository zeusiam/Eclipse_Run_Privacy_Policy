# Eclipse Run — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 20 June 2026 · **Developer:** Veratus Games · **Contact:** hello@veratusgroup.com

## Summary
Eclipse Run is a free game. We do not sell your personal data and there are no in-app purchases. The
game is supported by advertising. Depending on your choices and region, our advertising partner may
process limited device data to show and measure ads.

## What we store on your device
Game settings and progress (best distance, daily streak, owned cosmetics, audio/haptics/music
preferences, consent choices) are stored **only on your device** and are not transmitted to us. We
operate no servers and have no accounts. Leaderboards and achievements, where you opt in, are handled
by **Apple Game Center** under Apple's privacy policy.

## Advertising partner — Unity Ads
Eclipse Run is free and supported by **rewarded video ads** (you choose to watch one for a second
chance or to double your motes) and occasional full-screen ads between runs. These are served by
**Unity Ads** (Unity Technologies). To show and measure ads, Unity may process:

- **Advertising / device identifiers** (e.g. IDFA on iOS, Android Advertising ID) and coarse
  device/technical data, to show ads, cap frequency, and measure performance;
- **Ad-interaction data** (impressions, completions, clicks).

Unity's privacy practices are described here:
<https://unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-developer-privacy-policy>. This build integrates **no
third-party analytics SDK**.

## Your choices
- **iOS — App Tracking Transparency:** on first launch we ask permission to track for personalised
  advertising. If you decline, we do not use the advertising identifier for cross-app tracking; you
  may still see non-personalised ads.
- **Regional consent (GDPR/CCPA):** where required, you can withdraw consent; ads then run in a
  non-personalised mode.
- **Device controls:** you can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings.

## Children
Eclipse Run is not directed to children under the age of digital consent in your region.

## Data retention & deletion
On-device data is removed when you delete the app. For any partner-processed data, deletion requests
can be made via the contact address above and will be forwarded to the relevant processor.

## Changes
We will update this policy and the "Last updated" date as the game and its partners change.
